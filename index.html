Why waste time downloading TV NZB files when it can be done for you?

Leechr will check your myepisodes.com page for the shows you need to acquire and will automatically download the relevant NZB files from your favourite NZB sites.

Works great with a news-reader that can auto-import NZB files from a directory.

Leechr features include:

  • Automatic searching and retrieval of NZB files from:
    • NZBNDX.com
    • Newshost.co.za
    • Sickbeard.com
    • nzbx.co
    • nzb.cc
    • Usenet-Crawler.com
    • nzbclub.com
    • nzbs.org
    • and your own Newznab+ site!
  • Automatically mark episodes 'acquired' on myepisodes.com.
  • Video-file verification, no more passworded downloads.
  • Automatic Update of Leechr and helpers.dat
  • Platform Independent, only requirement is Python 2.6+
